ABOUT ME · UC SAN DIEGO

Hi, I'm Todd.
I build at the intersection of data, math, and autonomy.

I'm a second-year student at UC San Diego pursuing a double major in Data Science and Applied Mathematics. I enjoy turning mathematical ideas into systems that can learn, move, and make decisions.

My recent work spans humanoid reinforcement learning, motion-capture retargeting, variational image processing, and queueing models. At Tongji University's State Key Lab of AI Unmanned Systems, I worked with motion data and NVIDIA Isaac Sim; at UCSD, I helped students connect linear algebra theory with MATLAB computation.
